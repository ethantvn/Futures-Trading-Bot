# The picky bar (challenger acceptance criteria)

A challenger replaces or augments [[orb-w-incumbent]] only if ALL hold:

1. **WF MC pass ≥ ~70%** and **pass+payout ≥ ~52%** (current incumbent numbers — update here when they move)
2. **Density:** ≥ ~4–5 trades/month, ≥ 100 OOS trades
3. **Fold stability:** ≥ 50% positive folds (prefer ≥ 60%)
4. **Frozen 2026+ holdout:** no collapse; never promote on holdout alone (see the P17 stack trap)
5. **Costs:** $0.50/side + 1 tick slip; survives 2-tick stress
6. **Causal:** [[causal-only]]; ambiguity < ~10%

Known traps this bar exists to catch: sparsity mirages (P13 gold, P15 fvg_vol 7 trades, P17 only_inside 12 trades), holdout-only winners, lookahead.
